Love Books?

We do too! Here at The Book Club we are facinated to see what types of books each member is interested in reading. Whether you are into fiction, or the occasional rom-com, we believe that there is a book genre for everyone with a passion for reading.

Here at THE BOOK CLUB we challenge ourselves to be more open minded when it comes to book genres we typically wouldn't pick up at first glance. Our team leaders stay on top of book trends and keep an open mind when being recommended a book by our Book Club members. Whether you want to find out other peoples opinions on a book you have just read, or are wanting to see what the reviews are like on current books, THE BOOK CLUB is the place for you!

Our main goal here at the Book Club is finding you with fantastic books that you would enjoy reading. By joining us here at the Book Club, not only do you get reviews on the newest books to hit the store shelfs, but you also get a chance to read books in different genres with our BOOK CLUB
